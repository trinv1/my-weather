About



My Weather is an app for you to access real time weather information at any time from your device


Home Page

From the search bar at the top of the home page, type in any location anywhere in the world and
you will be able to view what the weather is like
at that location.

We show you the daily temperature of your chosen location, what the temperature feels like,
and details on weather specifics such as,

Humidity
Visibility
Pressure
Wind Speed


Settings

In the Settings page you are given a choice on whether you would like to set your temperature
unit as Celcius, Fahrenheit or Kelvin. Click save
and your preferred unit will be set.
